## What is SecureLoad?

**SecureLoad Systems Pvt. Ltd.** is a maritime technology company developing a unified technical ecosystem for cargo planning and execution — bringing shipboard loading computers, office-based planning tools, and operational intelligence into a single, consistent framework.

SecureLoad is a joint venture between **Admaren Tech Pvt. Ltd.** (India) and **Hoppe Marine GmbH** (Germany), combining Admaren's digital innovation and rapid product development with Hoppe Marine's 75+ years of precision marine automation expertise.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan — Establish a technically grounded cargo plan">
    Planning begins before the vessel is involved. Commercial and project management teams plan cargo intake, stowage layouts, and loading concepts using approved vessel models accessible from shore. Answer practical questions early: Can this cargo physically fit? Are access and clearances viable? Does the cargo sequence make sense across multiple ports?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify — Validate feasibility while planning">
    As cargo layouts and loading sequences are developed, the system verifies hydrostatic stability, longitudinal strength, draft limits, accelerations, and structural response. If a plan cannot remain compliant throughout the operation, it is identified immediately — not at the berth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute — Onboard execution">
    The approved loading plan prepared ashore is directly available onboard through Fleet Connect. The crew executes cargo operations confidently, with stability and strength verified at every step of the operation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor — Real-time monitoring">
    Live sensor data — tank levels, draft readings, ballast data — flows from the vessel to shore through Fleet Connect. Shore teams see what the crew has loaded. Plans and actuals stay aligned automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who It Is Built For

| Desk | Roles | How SecureLoad Helps |
|---|---|---|
| **Freight Forwarding** | Freight Forwarders, Project Logistics Coordinators | Early-stage cargo feasibility — evaluate whether cargo can be transported before vessel commitment |
| **Chartering** | Chartering Managers, Executives | Evaluate cargo distribution, ballast requirements, and verify that a technically compliant loading condition exists |
| **Operations** | Operations Managers, Fleet Operations Teams | Re-check feasibility as conditions change; monitor the loading condition throughout the operation |
| **Engineering** | Naval Architects, Structural Engineers | Routine stability and strength checks shift into the planning workflow; focus on complex and non-standard scenarios |
| **Project Management** | Supercargo, Heavy Lift Coordinator | Direct visibility into how cargo decisions affect the vessel; escalate only complex scenarios to engineering |
| **Vessel Crew** | Master, Chief Officer, Deck Officers | Receive the approved plan from shore, execute with confidence — no manual re-entry, no re-calculation |
